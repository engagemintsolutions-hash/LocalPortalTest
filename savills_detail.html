A hugely impressive family home, finished to a fabulous standard set in 4.61 acres with stunning gardens and grounds.

About this property

  • Wild Cherry Farm is a immaculately finished home filled with character through exposed beams and timber. It is finished to the highest standard with particularly high tech, blending a sense of modern and comfort.

    The ground floor comprises of a newly renovated open plan kitchen/reception room, which is the heart of this home. The space boasts tremendous proportions, with plenty of entertaining and hosting space for guests. The room is filled with light, creating a specious open feeling through the generous sized windows and French doors which overlook the idyllic gardens.

    The kitchen is fitted with wall and base units, providing ample storage. Along with integrated appliances, including a Falcon range cooker (available by separate negotiation), Siemens oven, a steam oven and a warming drawer. A hidden door blending seamlessly, shows a pantry area and a separate utility room with a sink and dishwasher.

    The sitting room holds a Jotul wood burning stove inside a large brick fireplace. There are French doors leading out to a terrace area, perfect for al-fresco dining. The boot room is opposite, which offers lots of storage and units housing washing facilities. As well as a shower room, there is also a gym, a further laundry room and a study which has plumbing on the ground floor.

    The main entrance hall boasts the grand staircase to the first floor, but through the cosy sitting room with exposed beams and a wood burning stove, there is a secondary hallway. This leads to the gorgeous principal bedroom, with outstanding views to the gardens. There is also a fabulous freestanding bath in the room, and an en suite shower room and walk in dressing room with storage. The hallway leads with stairs to a drawing room, which is an ideal entertaining space, with a beautiful vaulted ceiling and can be used for a variety of possibilities.

    The first floor holds three spacious double bedrooms, with one having an en suite shower room, and there is a newly fitted modern bathroom.


    Outside

    Wild Cherry Farm holds exceptional gardens, and has views towards the Mortimer Forest. The views can be seem from inside the property to the countryside and beyond. Approached down a private driveway with power for electric gates, leading to a parking area for several vehicles. Landscaped gardens and grounds which is mainly laid to lawn and set in around 4.61 acres, and has some well stocked herbaceous borders, a number of mature trees and shrubbery.

    There is also a picturesque lake within the grounds, and a wonderful kitchen garden to the rear which is well stocked with vegetable raised beds, a rose garden, a poly tunnel and greenhouses/sheds. There is also power to the kitchen, which is ideal for when guests are over in the warm summer months for outside dining. Along with a fenced paddock, following from the gardens.


    Outbuildings

    There is a substantial steel framed outbuilding, which has been sub-divided into various areas, providing over 4,000 sq ft of space. There is also a useful hobby room with electric underfloor heating, and has lots of potential for various uses.

    To complete the outbuildings, there is a a newly fitted dog grooming room, which has a shower and space for drying. Within the large barn are various stores, providing exceptional storage and garaging.

Local information

  • Elton is located near Ludlow, a market town and civil parish in Shropshire, England. Several towns and villages are located near Elton, Herefordshire. For example, these are Wigmore and Aston-On-Clun, as well as Ludlow. The area has a church- St Mary the Virgin Church, as well as St Giles' Church which is located north of Elton.
  • Elton is connected to Ludlow via Killhorse Lane. Ludlow is approximately 4 miles north east of Elton, a 7 minute drive.
  • Mortimer Forest is 1.9 miles north of Elton, a 4 minute drive via Killhorse Lane. The forest provides a popular destination for both walking and cycling.
  • Ludlow is approximately 4 miles away offering a range of amenities including historic sites, markets, recreational facilities, shops and dining options.
  • The nearest train station would be Ludlow train station located approximately 5.5 miles away. It provides train journeys to various destinations such as Shrewsbury, Cardiff Central and Manchester Piccadilly.
  • Elton attracts wildlife due to its diverse habitats like grasslands, arable land and woodland, which are home to various bird and animal species.
  • There are nearby schools such as Moor Park, Overton School, Bishop Hooper C E Primary School. Ludlow is home to many schools such as Saint Laurence C of E School, Busy Bodies Nursery, Ludlow Primary School and Shropshire County Primary Care Trust.
  • The nearest major road would be the A49 which provides access to neighbouring towns such as Shrewsbury.
