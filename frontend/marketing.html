FAQ

Knock Knock. Who's There?

Answers delivered right to your doorstep

How does the AI generate listing descriptions?

Our AI analyzes your property photos to identify rooms, features, and selling points. It then generates compelling, accurate descriptions in your chosen tone and style. Every description is checked against ASA compliance rules before delivery. You can edit and refine the output at any time.

Can I customize the brochure templates with my branding?

Absolutely! Upload your logo, set your brand colors, choose your fonts, and we'll apply them across all templates automatically. Your brochures will look like they were designed by your own marketing team — because they were, with AI assistance.

How does the social media automation work?

Connect your social accounts once, then Doorstep handles everything. We auto-generate platform-optimized posts (different formats for Instagram, Facebook, LinkedIn, X), schedule them at optimal times, and track performance. You approve before publishing, or set it to autopilot.

Is the content compliant with ASA and property advertising rules?

Yes. Every piece of content is automatically scanned for compliance issues — misleading claims, superlatives without evidence, and restricted terms. We flag potential issues and suggest compliant alternatives. You'll never accidentally publish something that could get you in trouble.

How accurate is the AI at identifying property features?

Our vision AI achieves 95%+ accuracy on room identification and feature detection. It can identify everything from kitchen styles to flooring types, garden features, and architectural details. You can always review and adjust the detected features before generating content.

Can multiple agents use the same account?

Yes! Our Professional and Enterprise plans support team access. Each agent gets their own login, can manage their own listings, while branch managers can oversee everything. Analytics are available at both agent and branch level.

What portals can I export listings to?

We support one-click export to Rightmove, Zoopla, OnTheMarket, Primelocation, and more. Listings are automatically formatted to meet each portal's requirements. We also integrate with major CRMs like Reapit, Alto, Dezrez, and Street.co.uk.

Do you offer a free trial?

Yes! Start with 3 free property listings — no credit card required. Experience the full workflow, generate real brochures, and see the quality for yourself. When you're ready, upgrade to Professional for unlimited access.
