Cookie Policy

Last updated: 25 November 2024

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device (computer, tablet, smartphone) when you visit a website. They help websites remember your preferences, improve functionality, and analyze usage patterns.

This Cookie Policy explains how Doorstep Solutions ("we", "us", "our") uses cookies and similar technologies on our website and platform.

2. Why We Use Cookies

We use cookies to:

  • Essential Functions: Enable core features like login sessions and security
  • Performance: Analyze how users interact with our platform to improve it
  • Personalization: Remember your preferences and settings
  • Analytics: Understand usage patterns and popular features
  • Security: Detect and prevent fraudulent activity

3. Types of Cookies We Use

3.1 Strictly Necessary Cookies

These cookies are essential for the website to function and cannot be switched off. They include:

Cookie Name Purpose Duration
session_id Maintains your logged-in state Session (deleted when browser closes)
csrf_token Security protection against cross-site request forgery Session
cookie_consent Stores your cookie preference choices 12 months

3.2 Performance & Analytics Cookies

These help us understand how visitors use our website so we can improve it. All data is aggregated and anonymous.

Cookie Name Provider Purpose Duration
_ga Google Analytics Distinguishes unique users 2 years
_ga_* Google Analytics 4 Tracks session data 2 years
_gid Google Analytics Distinguishes users within 24 hours 24 hours
_gat Google Analytics Throttles request rate 1 minute

3.3 Functional Cookies

These enable enhanced functionality and personalization.

Cookie Name Purpose Duration
user_preferences Remembers your display settings (theme, units, etc.) 6 months
recent_searches Stores your recent property searches for quick access 30 days
dashboard_layout Saves your customized dashboard configuration 12 months

3.4 Targeting/Advertising Cookies

We do NOT currently use advertising cookies. If we introduce them in the future, we will:

  • Obtain your explicit consent
  • Update this Cookie Policy
  • Provide opt-out mechanisms

4. Third-Party Cookies

Some cookies are set by third-party services we use:

4.1 Google Analytics

  • Purpose: Website traffic analysis and user behavior insights
  • Data Collected: Pages visited, time on site, device type, location (city-level)
  • Privacy Policy: Google Privacy Policy
  • Opt-out: Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on

4.2 Stripe (Payment Processing)

  • Purpose: Securely process payments and prevent fraud
  • Cookies: __stripe_sid, __stripe_mid
  • Privacy Policy: Stripe Privacy Policy

4.3 AWS CloudFront (Content Delivery)

  • Purpose: Deliver website content efficiently and securely
  • Cookies: CloudFront-* (for load balancing and security)
  • Privacy Policy: AWS Privacy Notice

5. How to Manage Cookies

5.1 Cookie Consent Banner

When you first visit our website, a banner will appear asking for your cookie preferences. You can:

  • Accept All: Allow all cookies (recommended for best experience)
  • Reject Optional: Only allow strictly necessary cookies
  • Customize: Choose which cookie categories to enable

5.2 Browser Settings

You can also control cookies through your browser settings:

  • Google Chrome: Settings > Privacy and Security > Cookies
  • Firefox: Settings > Privacy & Security > Cookies and Site Data
  • Safari: Preferences > Privacy > Manage Website Data
  • Microsoft Edge: Settings > Cookies and Site Permissions

Note: Blocking all cookies may prevent some website features from working properly.

5.3 Change Your Preferences

To update your cookie preferences after initial choice:

  • Click "Cookie Settings" in the footer of any page
  • Or clear your browser cookies and reload the page (banner will reappear)

6. Do Not Track (DNT)

Some browsers support "Do Not Track" signals. We respect DNT settings and will not track users who have it enabled (except for essential cookies required for site functionality).

7. Cookies and Personal Data

Some cookies may collect personal data (e.g., IP address, unique identifiers). This data is processed according to our Privacy Policy and UK GDPR regulations.

We ensure:

  • Data is stored securely and anonymized where possible
  • Third-party processors comply with data protection laws
  • You can request deletion of cookie data (subject to legal retention requirements)

8. Cookie Retention

Cookies are retained for the durations specified in the tables above. After expiration:

  • Session cookies are automatically deleted when you close your browser
  • Persistent cookies remain until expiry date unless manually deleted
  • Analytics data is anonymized after 26 months

9. International Users

If you access our website from outside the UK:

  • Cookie data may be transferred to/from the UK
  • We ensure adequate data protection safeguards (e.g., Standard Contractual Clauses)
  • Your rights under UK GDPR still apply

10. Children's Privacy

Our services are not intended for individuals under 18. We do not knowingly collect data from children via cookies.

11. Updates to This Policy

  • New cookie technologies or third-party services
  • Changes in legal requirements
  • Improvements to our website

Material changes will be notified via banner or email. "Last updated" date at the top reflects the most recent revision.
