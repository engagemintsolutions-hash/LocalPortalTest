Why Data Depth Matters

Basic AVMs use 3-5 data sources: price history, property type, and postcode. We use 38+ because property value isn't determined by price history alone. Schools, transport, crime, planning restrictions, environmental risks, and hyperlocal demographics all influence what buyers will pay—and what lenders can justify in audits.

Property Transaction Data

Every UK property sale and market activity since 1995

Land Registry Price Paid Data (PPD)

30+ million property transactions since 1995. Every freehold and leasehold sale in England and Wales.

  • Coverage: 1.2 GB indexed by postcode and county
  • Update Frequency: Monthly
  • Authority: HM Land Registry
  • Use Case: Comparable sales analysis, price trend modeling

UK House Price Index (HPI)

Official house price statistics by property type and region.

  • Coverage: Regional trends by detached, semi, terraced, flat
  • Update Frequency: Monthly
  • Authority: ONS, HM Land Registry, Registers of Scotland, Land & Property Services NI
  • Use Case: Market trend analysis, regional adjustments

Energy Performance Certificates (EPC)

118 local authority datasets covering every domestic EPC rating in the UK.

  • Coverage: 1.8 GB of energy ratings, floor areas, construction dates
  • Update Frequency: Quarterly
  • Authority: Department for Energy Security and Net Zero
  • Use Case: Property attributes, energy efficiency premiums

Location & Boundary Data

Hyperlocal geography from Output Areas to Local Authorities

Office for National Statistics Postcode Directory (ONSPD)

Complete UK postcode to geographic area mappings.

  • Coverage: 1.8 million postcodes mapped to Output Areas, LSOAs, MSOAs
  • Granularity: Output Area level (~300 people each)
  • Authority: Office for National Statistics
  • Use Case: Hyperlocal area attribution, boundary matching

Administrative Boundaries

Official geographic boundaries for all UK statistical areas.

  • Coverage: Output Areas, LSOAs, MSOAs, Wards, Local Authorities
  • Authority: ONS Geography
  • Use Case: Spatial analysis, area classification

INSPIRE Polygons

Geospatial datasets for infrastructure and land use.

  • Coverage: Planning zones, conservation areas, protected land
  • Authority: Various government departments
  • Use Case: Planning restriction identification

Demographics & Population Data

Census 2021 and Index of Multiple Deprivation statistics

Census 2021 (Output Area Level)

Complete UK census data at 300-person neighborhood granularity.

  • Coverage: 63 MB covering age, ethnicity, housing tenure, education, employment
  • Granularity: Output Area (smallest UK geography)
  • Authority: Office for National Statistics
  • Use Case: Demographic profiling, household composition analysis

Index of Multiple Deprivation (IMD)

Official measure of relative deprivation across England.

  • Coverage: Income, employment, education, health, crime, housing, environment
  • Authority: Ministry of Housing, Communities & Local Government
  • Use Case: Socioeconomic context, area desirability scoring

Employment Statistics

Business counts and jobs density by area.

  • Coverage: Business counts by LSOA, jobs density by MSOA
  • Authority: Office for National Statistics
  • Use Case: Economic activity indicators, employment accessibility
